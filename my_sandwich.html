My Favorite Ham-Turkey Melt Sandwich


Below are the ingredients to make this sandwich:


  1. 4 slices of deli-style ham slices
  2. 4 slickes of deli-style turkey slices
  3. 2 long slices of pickle
  4. 3 slices of onion
  5. chopped romaine lettuce
  6. 1 tablespoon of mustard
  7. 5 hot peppers
  8. 3 slices of tomatoes
  9. 3 slices of provolone cheese
  10. 2 slices of rye bread










Spread mustard on rye bread, add all the above ingredients on top on one slice of bread. Put the provolone cheese on last and put other slice on top. Place in microwave for 45 seconds.
